ABOUT US

The Bosphorus Experience

Bosphorus VA is a Turkey-based VATSIM virtual airline founded by aviation enthusiasts who believe simulated flight should be as close to the real thing as possible.

We operate from Istanbul's LTFM across Europe, the Middle East, Africa and Asia — using a diverse fleet ranging from nimble ATR turboprops to long-haul widebodies.
